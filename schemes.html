How to Apply for Schemes

Step 1: Check Eligibility

Verify if you meet the scheme's criteria including landholding size, income level, and other requirements.

Step 2: Gather Documents

Collect necessary documents like Aadhaar card, land records, bank details, and identity proof.

Step 3: Visit Portal/Office

Apply online through the official portal or visit your nearest agriculture department office.

Step 4: Submit Application

Fill the application form accurately and submit with all required documents.

Step 5: Track Status

Use your application ID to track the status online or through helpline numbers.
