Maharashtra Farming Guide

1. Land Preparation (Pre-Monsoon)

Deep plowing in April-May, add FYM 15-20 days before monsoon onset.

2. Seed Selection

Use certified seeds from MAHABEEJ or local approved varieties.

3. Sowing with Monsoon

Time sowing with first monsoon showers (June 15 - July 15).

4. Irrigation Management

Critical irrigation at flowering & grain filling stages.

5. Nutrient Management

Soil test based fertilization, micronutrient application in deficient areas.

6. Pest Management

IPM approach, use of pheromone traps, neem-based pesticides.

7. Harvesting

Harvest at proper maturity, avoid pre-monsoon rains for rabi crops.

8. Post-Harvest

Proper drying, storage in moisture-proof containers, market linkage.

Sugarcane - Maharashtra Guide
Main Growing Regions: Western Maharashtra (Pune, Kolhapur, Satara)

Planting Seasons: Adsali (June-July), Pre-seasonal (Oct-Nov), Suru (Jan-Feb)

Soil: Deep, well-drained loamy soil with good water holding capacity

Maharashtra Varieties: Co 86032, CoM 0265, Co 0238

Water Requirement: 2000-2500 mm annually, critical stages: germination, tillering, grand growth

Yield: 80-120 tons/ha (Western Maharashtra), 60-80 tons/ha (Other regions)

Government Support: Subsidy on drip irrigation, interest subvention on loans
