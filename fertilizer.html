Urea (46-0-0)

High nitrogen content, promotes vegetative growth

  • 46% Nitrogen
  • Best for: Leafy vegetables, cereals
  • Application: Basal and top dressing
üåº
DAP (18-46-0)

High phosphorus, good for root development

  • 18% N, 46% P
  • Best for: Flowering plants, fruits
  • Application: Basal application
üçé
MOP (0-0-60)

High potassium, improves fruit quality

  • 60% Potassium
  • Best for: Fruits, vegetables
  • Application: Split application
üåø
Complex (NPK)

Balanced nutrient mix

  • Various ratios (10-26-26, 12-32-16)
  • Best for: All crops
  • Application: Basal and top dressing

How to properly test your soil for accurate fertilizer recommendations

1
Sample Collection

Collect soil samples from 10-15 spots at 15cm depth

2
Mix & Dry

Mix samples thoroughly and air dry in shade

3
Package Properly

Pack 500g of soil in clean bag with field details

4
Submit to Lab

Submit to nearest soil testing laboratory

5
Get Report

Receive detailed soil health card with recommendations

6
Implement

Apply fertilizers as per recommendations
